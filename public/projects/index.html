Projects

Here is a brief list of things that I currently am/have worked on. Genuinely thankful to have had these opportunities. I’m always open to more opportunities, so if anything I’ve worked on interests you, please feel free to get in touch!

Currently

I’m currently working primarily on independent projects and supporting speech in Oxford. My time is generally split between the following:

  • Working on convening the Oxford Women and Gender Minorities’ Open, to be held in November 2023! If you’re interested, you can sign-up here.
  • Reading about climate justice and international climate politics, currently working on The International Politics of the Environment by Hurrell and Kingsbury!
  • Being Director of Print at OSPL, and working out how to continue protecting print student publications.
  • Trying to not be computer illiterate, learning HTML/CSS and improving on R! (If you’re reading this website, at least half of this project has gone well).
  • Catching up on some summer rest.

Previously

  • Researcher @ Righting History, exploring the vast amounts of untold history within Canada and the power behind who gets to tell your story.
  • Convenor of Oxford Schools, the largest high school level debating competition in the world in over 5 continents and 1500+ participants. Very thankful to have scaled this competition more internationally and expanding debate access in the UK.
  • Research Assistant @ Department of Economics, Oxford. Creating a system to catalogue and display the policy research and writings of the Department.
  • Worked as a consultant with the Oxford Strategy Group, advising a Fortune 500 company on how to comply with biodiversity regulation in the UK and Europe.
  • Research Micro-Intern @ Faculty of English Language and Literature, Oxford, researching parliamentary culture in the 1600s.
